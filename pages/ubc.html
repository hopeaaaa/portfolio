UBC Pharmacists Clinic

Designing interfaces that simplify complexity and support patient-centered care.

Overview

CLIENT

The UBC Pharmacists Clinic

ROLE

Service Designer and UX Lead; I facilitated stakeholder engagement, led the research and prototyping process, and supported the clinic through technology integration.

PROBLEM

Clinic operations relied heavily on manual processes, creating administrative burdens and inefficiencies for pharmacists and staff.

SOLUTION

A suite of digital tools and service improvements to reduce administrative workload, improve communication, and enhance the overall patient care experience.

Impact

  • Led redesign of 5+ core clinical processes, including appointment booking, patient communication, and EMR workflows; improving efficiency and reducing administrative load.
  • Developed and implemented a new patient intake system that decreased appointment booking time by 30% and minimized back-and-forth between staff and patients.
  • Reduced appointment no-show rates by 25% through implementation of improved reminder and confirmation workflows.
  • Introduced 3 new tools for virtual patient support, video conferencing, and appointment confirmations, improving continuity of care during remote visits.
  • Integrated WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards into all digital touchpoints to support inclusive access for patients with disabilities.
  • Mentored pharmacy students and clinical staff on human-centered design practices to support long-term innovation in care delivery.

Projects

The UBC Pharmacists Clinic is a university-affiliated, pharmacist-led patient care clinic. At the Clinic, the clinicians use their specialized knowledge in medication to support the care of medically complex patients.

As Healthcare Designer for the clinic, I worked to improve the clinical processes and patient experience by integrating new technologies and streamlining digital interactions. I led the service and UX design processes, collaborating closely with clinicians, administrators, and external stakeholders to deliver solutions. My contributions spanned research, design, implementation, and mentorship, resulting in a more efficient and user-friendly experience for patients and staff alike.
